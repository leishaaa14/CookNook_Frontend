About the Project

Welcome to CookNook! This project is designed to help users explore delicious recipes with ease. Simply enter the ingredients you have, and let our platform find amazing recipes for you. Whether you're a novice cook or a culinary expert, CookNook is your companion in the kitchen.

With a user-friendly interface and the power of modern APIs, CookNook ensures that finding your next meal is as delightful as cooking it.
